A bit about us...

Step into MALON, where jewelry dances to the rhythm of techno and electronic music. Crafted from bolts, chainmail, and stainless steel, our pieces exude raw energy and rave culture. Embrace your untamed spirit with our edgy designs and let your style speak volumes without saying a word.

OUR PRODUCTS

Explore our exclusive metal art collection: BOLT collection, a fusion of industrial bolts and stainless steel; CHAIN MAIL collection, where we craft intricate patterns using stainless steel washers. Bold, edgy, and uniquely designed, each piece embodies the spirit of creativity and individuality; BUNDLES, matching sets of necklace or bracelet with earrings. Find your unique expression in each piece.
